The Story Behind La Llave

Not a Tour.
An Invitation.

I'm Lucas — 23, born and raised in Hong Kong to a Spanish mother and a Dutch father. After years between Switzerland and the Netherlands, I arrived in Madrid and knew immediately this was home. A lifetime in international settings across three continents taught me one thing: the best moments happen around a table, a glass of wine, or a conversation with someone who truly knows the place.

I'm someone who lives for good food — I cook, I eat, I obsess over where the best tortilla in the city is this week. I'm drawn to sports, from football matches under floodlights to early morning padel. And I collect the kind of places that don't end up in guidebooks: the wine cellars that don't appear on Google, the bars without signs, the restaurants where the chef only cooks for people he knows.

La Llave is how I share that Madrid with you. Every experience is personally organized and guided by me. I'll arrange a private driver, take you to the places I've built real relationships with, and tell you the stories behind every stop — from the provenance of a painting at the Prado to why a particular cocktail bar only opens on Tuesdays.

A private cochinillo lunch in Segovia with a scenic drive through the countryside. Seats at the Bernabéu or the Metropolitano on match day. An evening that begins with a sunset wine tasting on a rooftop and ends wherever the night takes us. Whatever the occasion, I'll make it happen — personally and with care.

This isn't a tour. It's what it feels like when a well-connected friend who knows every corner of Madrid invites you into his city — because that's exactly what it is.
